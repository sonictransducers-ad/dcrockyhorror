48th Anniversary Spectacular Tour

Tuesday October 31th, 2023

8:00pm at The Lincoln Theatre

We've got an EXTRA SPECIAL HALLOWEEN SHOW! Join us, Barry Bostwick (the original 'BRAD MAJORS'), and Patricia Quinn (the original 'MAGENTA') at the historic Lincoln Theatre in Washington DC on Tuesday, October 31st. There will be a memorabilia display, costume contest, and of course, audience participation! Tickets are available through TicketMaster.com, TheLincolnDC.com, and IMPConcerts.com.
