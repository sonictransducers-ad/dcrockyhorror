Location

Located in the heart of downtown DC, Landmark Theatre's E Street Cinema specializes in independent and foreign language films, documentary features and classic revivals. It's accessible by all five lines on the Metrorail system, and was named "Best Movie Theatre" by the Washington City Paper.

The Sonic Transducers perform as part of E Street's cine insomnia series; midnight screenings ranging from classic anime and horror to sci-fi favorites, offbeat musicals, cult sensations and more!

Getting There

Parking

The cinema offers parking at a discounted rate in the adjacent garage (see details on their site).

Note: This parking garage closes at 2:30 am. Your car must be removed prior to this time or will be locked in the garage overnight.

Street parking is free after 10:00 PM.

Public Transportation

Metro accessible from the Blue, Green, Orange, Red, and Yellow lines at the Metro Center, Gallery Place, or Chinatown metro stops.

Note: the metro closes at 1 AM on both Friday and Saturday. This is after the end of the show, so you will need an alternate way home.
