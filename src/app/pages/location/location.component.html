Location

8 Screens, Reserved Seating, Premium Concessions. The full-service bar features a wide variety of beer, wine and liquor including many local brews and film-themed cocktails. Bethesda Row Cinema features a mix of independent film, foreign language cinema and Hollywood favorites.

Getting There

Parking

Free parking is available after 10pm in several nearby parking garages, lots, and street parking spots.

Public Transportation

Metro accessible from the Red line at the Bethesda metro stop.

Note: the metro closes at 1 AM on both Friday and Saturday. This is after the end of the show, so you will need an alternate way home.
