Join Us

Is your biggest dream to run around in a corset while pretending to be someone else? Have those lips been part of your dreams (or nightmares) your whole life? Is the sweet sound of Richard O'Brien's music the soundtrack to your existence? Are you over 18?

Then come join our cast!

You'll need to have a flexible schedule (second weekend of every month and occasional weeknights), work hard, pledge allegiance to the lips, and have fun!

We are looking for both actors and tech crew! No experience is required to join (either Rocky Horror or theatre related). However, you will need to do two shows as a techie (lights, props, etc.) before becoming a full member. After that, you can audition for any role that interests you or keep being a techie behind the scenes; whatever floats your boat!
