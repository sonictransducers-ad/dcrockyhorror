Phantom of the Paradise

The Sonic Transducers have thrilled/chilled/fulfilled you with The Rocky Horror Picture Show. You've seen us pull our wigs off the shelf for Hedwig and the Angry Inch. Now we're selling our soul for Rock and Roll with Phantom of the Paradise!

A rock musical about a rock opera with music by Paul Williams (yes, that Paul Williams), Phantom of the Paradise is what happens when you combine Faust and Phantom of the Opera with a splash of Frankenstein and then cover it all with a blanket of glitter and cocaine! This is also the debut movie for Jessica Harper, who later played Janet in Rocky's sequel, Shock Treatment!
