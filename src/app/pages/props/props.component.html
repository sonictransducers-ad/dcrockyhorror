Prop Guide

If you buy a prop bag from us before the show, congratulations! You get to throw things! Here's a detailed look at what's in your Sonic Transducers prop bag and how to use it. Remember, always throw props up and back, never at the cast.

1. Newspaper

When Brad and Janet get caught in the rain, hold up your newspaper! You're about to get wet!

2. Glove

When Frank gives his creation speech, snap your glove along with him!

3. Noisemaker

When the creation speech is finished, use your noisemaker to celebrate!

4. Toilet Paper

After Dr. Scott arrives, throw your toilet paper in the air when Brad says "Great Scott!"

5. "Toast"

When Frank proposes a toast, follow suit and throw your cardboard "toast" in the air!

6. Birthday Hat

Sing Happy Birthday to Rocky! Put on your birthday hat when Frank does!

7. Playing Cards

When Frank sings "Cards for sorrow, cards for pain", throw your playing cards in the air!
